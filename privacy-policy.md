This page explains how this site (DOTP — `jaychung0302.github.io`) handles
information collected from visitors. This is a personal technical blog operated
by Nate (Jisu Chung).

## Information this site collects

This site does not require account registration and does not ask visitors to
submit personal information. Standard server logs and analytics tools may
record technical information such as:

- browser type and version
- device and operating system information
- referring page (the page that linked you here)
- access time and approximate location derived from IP address
- pages viewed on this site

This data is used only to understand site usage in aggregate and to improve
content quality.

## Analytics

This site uses **Google Analytics (GA4)** to measure traffic. Google Analytics
sets cookies and collects the technical information listed above. You can
disable Google Analytics by installing the
[Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

## Advertising — Google AdSense

This site uses, or may use, **Google AdSense** to display advertisements on
article (post) pages.

- Google and its third-party vendors use cookies to serve ads based on a
  visitor's prior visits to this site or other sites.
- Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve
  ads to visitors based on their visits to this site and/or other sites on
  the Internet.
- Third-party vendors and ad networks may use cookies, web beacons, or
  similar technologies to collect information when ads are served.
- You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting
  [Google Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads) or
  [aboutads.info](https://www.aboutads.info/choices/).

For more information about how Google uses data from sites that use its
services, see
[How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites).

## Comments

Article pages may include a comment section provided by **Disqus**. If you
post a comment, the data you submit is handled according to
[Disqus's Privacy Policy](https://help.disqus.com/en/articles/1717103-disqus-privacy-policy).

## Cookies

Cookies set on this site come from the third-party services listed above
(Google Analytics, Google AdSense, and Disqus). Most browsers allow you to
review and delete cookies via their settings, and to block third-party cookies
entirely.

## External links

This site contains links to external websites. This privacy policy applies
